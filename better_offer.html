Purpose

Make biological data reusable by labeling it better.

Many GenBank / NCBI samples have incomplete/missing metadata (country, sample type, optional ethnicity/specific location). This tool helps researchers generate clean, structured labels — ready for papers, datasets, or analysis.

This is not a black-box AI. It’s a partnership between AI speed and human expertise.

What you get

  • AI-powered inference from GenBank accession alone.
  • Country + Sample Type by default; optional labels (e.g. ethnicity & specific location) on request.
  • Transparent outputs: explanations, citations.
  • Excel export; batch upload; multi-ID input.
  • Human-in-the-loop control: 1-click feedback ensures you decide what counts.
Positioning: This tool is an accelerator, not a replacement. AI surfaces leads quickly → Human Intelligence validates tricky cases.

Edge Cases (our specialty)

Some samples are especially hard to label because they don’t have a DOI, PubMed ID, or linked article. Normally these are ignored or left as “unknown.” We call them edge cases.

  • Priced the same as normal runs ($0.10/sample) — no penalty for difficulty
  • Custom labels (e.g. ethnicity, city/province) can also be applied to edge cases on request
Why it matters: One early researcher tested 4,932 samples and found our predictions for some edge cases were more accurate than his manual annotations — even when metadata was missing.
